# Administer First Aid
#Actions/2 
#Ability-tag/Manipulate 

**Requirement:** You're holding or wearing _healer's tools_.

You perform first aid on a creature that is [[Dying]] or Bleeding (see Persistent Damage in the [[Gameplay Mind Map.canvas|Gameplay Mind Map]]). If the creature is afflicted by both, decide which one you want this action to affect before rolling. You may use this action again to deal with the other effect.
