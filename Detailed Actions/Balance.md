# Balance
#Actions/1 
#Ability-tag/Move 

**Requirements:** You are in a square with a narrow or uneven surface requiring you to balance.

Do an **Acrobatics check** against the surface's Balance DC. While on a surface requiring balance, you are [[Flat-footed]].

* **Critical success:** You move up to your speed.
* **Success:** You move up to your speed, treating the narrow/uneven ground as if it were [[Difficult Terrain]].
* **Failure:** You must remain stationary to keep your balance or you fall. If you fall, your turn ends.
* **Critical failure:** You fall and your turn ends.
