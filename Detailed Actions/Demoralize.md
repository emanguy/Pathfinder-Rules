# Demoralize
#Actions/1 
#Ability-tag/Auditory  #Ability-tag/Concentrate  #Ability-tag/Emotion  #Ability-tag/Fear #Ability-tag/Mental 

You try to shake an enemy's resolve. Choose a creature within **30 feet** of you that you're aware of, then attempt an **Intimidation check** against their **Will DC**.

If the target doesn't understand the language you speak, you take a **-4 circumstance penalty** on the check. Regardless of the result, the target is **immune to future demoralize attempts for 10 minutes**.

* **Critical Success:** The target becomes [[Frightened]] 2.
* **Success:** The target becomes [[Frightened]] 1.
