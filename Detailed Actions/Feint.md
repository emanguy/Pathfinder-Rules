# Feint
#Actions/1 
#Ability-tag/Mental 

**Requirement:** Trained in deception and you are within melee reach of the target you're attempting to feint.

You fake out your opponent with a flourish before your real attack. Roll a **deception check** against the target's **perception DC**.

* **Critical success:** You successfully feint and your target is [[Flat-footed]] against melee attacks you attempt against it until the end of your next turn.
* **Success:** You're partially successful. Your target is flat-footed against your next melee attack until the end of your turn.
* **Critical failure:** Your feint backfires and you're flat-footed against melee attacks the target makes against you until the end of your next turn.
