# Force Open
#Actions/1 
#Ability-tag/Attack 

You attempt to forcibly open a door/window/heavy gate/etc. Make an **Athletics check**.  **Without a crowbar**, take a -2 circumstance bonus to the roll.

* **Critical success:** You manage to open the door/window/etc. without damaging it.
* **Success:** You manage to open the door/window/etc. and it gains the [[Broken]] condition. If it's sturdy, the DM may just have it take damage.
* **Critical failure:** You jam the door/window/etc., causing a -2 circumstance bonus to future attempts to force it open.
