# Grapple
#Actions/1 
#Ability-tag/Attack 

**Requirement:** You have at least one free hand or have your target [[Grabbed]] or [[Restrained]]. Your target isn't more than 1 size larger than you.

Make an **Athletics check** against the creature or object's **Fortitude DC**.

* **Critical success:** You have your target [[Restrained]] until the end of your next turn, if you move away, or if the target gets away using [[Escape]].
* **Success:** You have your target [[Grabbed]] until the end of your next turn , if you move away, or if the target gets away using [[Escape]]
* **Failure:** You fail to grab your target. The Restrained or Grabbed conditions on them immediately end.
* **Critical failure:** The Restrained or Grabbed condition on the creature ends. They can then make you [[Grabbed]] or make you fall [[Prone]].
