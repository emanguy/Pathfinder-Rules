# Lie
#Ability-tag/Auditory 
#Ability-tag/Concentrate 
#Ability-tag/Linguistic 
#Ability-tag/Mental 
#Ability-tag/Secret 

You try to lie to someone. This takes **1 round** unless the lie is elaborate. Roll a **deception check** against the **perception DC** of each creature you try to fool. Your targets may get circumstance bonuses depending on how believable the lie is.
