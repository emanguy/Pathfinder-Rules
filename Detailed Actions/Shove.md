# Shove
#Actions/1 
#Ability-tag/Attack 

**Requirement:** You have at least one hand free and your target isn't more than 1 size larger than you.

Roll an **athletics check** against your target's **fortitude DC** to shove them away from you.

* **Critical success:** You push your target up to 10 feet away from you. You can [[Stride]] after it but you can only move the same distance and direction.
* **Success:** You push your target back 5 feet. You can Stride after them but you can only do so the same distance and direction.
* **Critical failure:** You lose you balance and fall [[Prone]].
