# Sneak
#Actions/1 
#Ability-tag/Move #Ability-tag/Secret 

Try to move to another place while remaining or becoming [[Undetected]]. [[Stride]] up to half your speed (incl. Burrowing, Climbing, Flying, or Swimming). If you're **undetected by a creature and it's impossible for it to see you** your critical failures become failures instead. You also continue to be undetected if you lose cover/greater cover or lose [[Concealed]].
