# Treat Poison
#Actions/1 
#Ability-tag/Manipulate 

**Requirement:** Trained in Medicine and you're holding or wearing _healer's tools_.

Perform a **Medicine check** against the poison's DC. Once you attempt to treat poison on a creature, you can't do it again until the creature makes another save against the poison.

* **Critical success:** You give the creature a **+4 circumstance bonus** to its next roll against the poison
* **Success:** You give the creature a **+2 circumstance bonus** to its next roll against the poison
* **Critical failure:** You give the creature a **-2 circumstance penalty** to its next roll against the poison
