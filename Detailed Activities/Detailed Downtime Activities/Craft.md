# Craft
#Ability-tag/Downtime
#Ability-tag/Manipulate 

**Requirements:** trained in crafting.

You need certain feats to craft items:
1. **Alchemical Crafting** for alchemical items
2. **Magical Crafting** for magic items
3. **Snare Crafting** for snares

There are requirements for crafting items:
* The item is **your level or lower**. An item not specifying a level is level 0.
	* If the item is **9th level or higher** you need to be a master in crafting
	* If the item is **16th level or higher** you need to be legendary in crafting
* You **have the formula** for the item
* You have the correct **set of tools and possibly a workshop** (e.g. a smithy for a metal shield or an alchemist's lab for alchemical items)
* You supply **raw materials worth at least half the item's price**. You always spend at least that amount on materials when crafting successfully. In a settlement, you can probably purchase raw materials you need unless they are rare
