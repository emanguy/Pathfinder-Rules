# Create Forgery
#Ability-tag/Downtime 
#Ability-tag/Secret 

**Requirements:** trained in society

When you create a forgery, you need the proper writing materials. The DM makes a **secret DC 20 Society check** on your behalf to determine if the forgery is passable at a first glance. If this check succeeds, a creature will only notice the forgery if they make an **active Perception or Society check against your Society DC**.
