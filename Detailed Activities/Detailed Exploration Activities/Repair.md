# Repair
#Ability-tag/Exploration 
#Ability-tag/Manipulate 

**Requirements:** You are holding or wearing a repair kit.

Make a **Crafting check** to spend 10 minutes attempting to fix a damaged item. The DC to repair is typically the same as the DC to craft the item in the first place. Destroyed items cannot be repaired.

* **Critical Success:** You restore 10HP to the item, plus an extra 10 per proficiency rank you have in crafting
* **Success:** You restore 5HP to the item, plus an extra 5 per proficiency rank you have in crafting
* **Critical failure:** You deal 2d6 damage to the item. Apply the item's hardness to this damage (see "item HP" in the [[Gameplay Mind Map.canvas|Gameplay Mind Map]])
