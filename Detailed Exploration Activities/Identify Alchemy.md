# Identify Alchemy
#Ability-tag/Concentrate 
#Ability-tag/Exploration 
#Ability-tag/Secret 

**Requirements:** You're holding or wearing _alchemist's tools_ and are trained in crafting.

Make a **Crafting check** to spend 10 minutes testing the nature of an alchemical item with your alchemist's tools. If your attempt is interrupted you must start over.

* **Success:** You identify the item and the means of activating it.
* **Failure:** You fail to identify the item but can try again.
* **Critical failure:** You misidentify the item as another of the DM's choice.
