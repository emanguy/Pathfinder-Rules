# Make an Impression
#Ability-tag/Auditory 
#Ability-tag/Concentrate 
#Ability-tag/Exploration 
#Ability-tag/Linguistic 
#Ability-tag/Mental 

Make a **Diplomacy check** within 1 minute of starting a conversation to try and make a good impression on someone to make them temporarily agreeable. The check is compared against the target's **Will DC**.

The result of your impression only lingers for the extent of the current conversation, typically.

* **Critical success:** The target's attitude toward you improves by 2 steps
* **Success:** The target's attitude toward you improves by 1 step
* **Critical failure:** The target's attitude toward you decreases by 1 step
