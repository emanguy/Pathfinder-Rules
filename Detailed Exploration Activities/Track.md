# Track
#Ability-tag/Concentrate 
#Ability-tag/Exploration 
#Ability-tag/Move 
#Actions/1 

You follow tracks up to half your Exploration Speed (see exploration section in [[Gameplay Mind Map.canvas|Gameplay Mind Map]]).

During exploration, each success will allow you to continue following the trail for an hour.

* **Success:** You find the trail or continue to follow it.
* **Failure:** You lose the trail but can try again after a 1-hour delay.
* **Critical failure:** You lose the trail and can't try again for 24 hours.
